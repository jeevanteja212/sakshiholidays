Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Dubai

Welcome to Dubai. Arrive at Dubai airport and transfer to hotel for your stay. In the evening, you may choose to visit some of the popular malls to shop and dine. In case, you wish to make the evening more interesting, then head to one of the several souks (open air markets) of the historic district of Bur Dubai. Overnight stay in Dubai.

Day 2: Dubai - City Tour & Dhow Dinner Cruise

After breakfast, proceed for half day city tour of Dubai. Visit the Dubai Museum, and stop to take pictures at Burj Al Arab, Jumeirah Mosque, Burj Khalifa and Dubai Mall, among others. Spend the evening cruising the Dubai Creek on a dhow cruise, with music and lights- a signature experience that cannot be missed while in Dubai. This is a one hour evening cruise that includes dinner on the Dhow itself. Overnight stay in Dubai.

Day 3: Dubai - Desert Safari

Morning is free for Shopping & leisure. Later in the afternoon you will get picked up from your hotel for the desert safari; a ride over the sand dunes followed by a barbeque dinner and belly dance performance in the desert- another Dubai essential! The Desert Safari begins early evening with dune bashing, and is followed by barbeque dinner at a traditional campsite. Overnight stay in Dubai.

Day 4: Dubai - Visit Burj Khalifa

Today enjoy your visit to Burj Khalifa 124th floor (non-peak hours). See the city of Dubai laid out in front of you from an unprecedented height from the observation deck. Enjoy unobstructed views of Dubai’s skyline from floor-to-ceiling glass walls, which offer great photo opportunities. Overnight stay in Dubai.

Day 5: Dubai - Departure

After breakfast it’s time to check out. Transfer to the airport for your onward flight or back home.
